Yes. Shrinklet supports common macOS-readable video files including MP4 and MOV.

Can I control video resolution?

Yes. Video settings include practical max-resolution and frame-rate options.

Is Shrinklet a full video editor?

No. Shrinklet focuses on compression, not timeline editing.
