Frequently asked questions

Is Shrinklet an online PDF compressor?

No. Shrinklet is a Mac app designed for local PDF compression instead of uploading private documents to a web tool.

Can Shrinklet compress scanned PDFs?

Yes, Shrinklet can help reduce many scanned PDFs, though results depend on the original file, images, and document structure.

Can I choose a smaller PDF size?

Yes. Use stronger presets or custom PDF/image settings when you need a smaller output file.
