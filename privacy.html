Privacy Policy for SqueezeIt

SqueezeIt is a macOS app designed to reduce the size of PDF documents and image files. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how SqueezeIt handles your data.

Effective Date May 3rd, 2026
Developer Venkata Palakodety / Jungle Pixels
App Name SqueezeIt PDF Compressor
Contact junglepixelsbiz@gmail.com

1. Data Collection

SqueezeIt does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share any personal information.

The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide personal details.

2. File Processing

SqueezeIt processes your selected files locally on your Mac.

Files you add to SqueezeIt, including PDFs and images, are used only for the purpose of compression. Your files are not uploaded to our servers, transmitted to third parties, or stored by Jungle Pixels.

3. Supported File Types

SqueezeIt may process files such as:

  • PDF documents
  • JPG images
  • PNG images
  • TIFF images
  • HEIC images
  • GIF images
  • WebP images

These files remain on your device unless you choose to share, move, or upload them using other apps or services outside of SqueezeIt.

4. Internet Access

SqueezeIt does not require an internet connection to compress files.

The app does not send your documents, images, filenames, or compression results to Jungle Pixels or any external server.

5. Analytics and Tracking

SqueezeIt does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, or behavioral profiling.

We do not track your usage across apps or websites.

6. Payments and App Store Data

If you download or purchase SqueezeIt through the Mac App Store, Apple may process certain information related to your download, purchase, subscription, refund, or device according to Apple’s own privacy policies.

Jungle Pixels does not receive your full payment details, credit card information, or Apple ID password.

7. Crash Reports and Diagnostics

Apple may provide developers with crash reports or diagnostic information if you have chosen to share analytics with app developers in your macOS settings.

These reports are provided by Apple and may help us improve app stability. They do not include the contents of your compressed files.

8. Children’s Privacy

SqueezeIt does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Because the app does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect data from users of any age.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Since SqueezeIt does not collect personal data, there is no personal data to share.

10. Data Retention

SqueezeIt does not retain your personal information or uploaded files.

Compressed files are saved only where you choose to save them on your Mac.

11. Security

Because files are processed locally on your Mac and are not uploaded to Jungle Pixels, your documents and images remain under your control.

You are responsible for managing access to your device, files, and backups.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Continued use of SqueezeIt after changes are posted means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
