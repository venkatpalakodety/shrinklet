5. Supported File Types

Shrinklet processes the following file types:

  • PDF documents
  • JPEG / JPG images
  • PNG images
  • TIFF images
  • HEIC images
  • GIF images
  • WebP images
  • Common video files that macOS can read, including MP4, M4V, MOV, and many AVI files
  • Common audio files that macOS can read, including MP3, M4A, AAC, WAV, AIFF, CAF, and similar formats

Supported formats may vary based on what your version of macOS can decode. These files remain on your device at all times. They are not uploaded, transmitted, or shared with Jungle Pixels, Venkata Palakodety, or any third party.
